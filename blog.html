School Prefects

The school prefects are the pupils governing body of St. Kizito Primary School. They serve the school community by providing an active and representative voice for pupils to influence and impact school decisions.

This council is central to leadership development, responsibility, and participation in school life.

Continuous Professional Development

SKIPS offers a wide range of continuous professional development programmes for staff.

We run face-to-face training with well-trained professionals in different fields to ensure professional and socioeconomic development of our staff.

School OBs and OGs

Maintaining a strong link for life, in Uganda and around the world, we warmly welcome our OBs and OGs to visit St. Kizito Primary School, Lira.

If you are not yet a member, register through the Alumni Community section and reconnect with a global network that keeps growing.

Our Objectives

We aim to strengthen relationships between School OBs and OGS, pupils, and soon-to-be OBs and OGs while maximizing potential in both personal and professional lives.

St. Kizito Primary School

St. Kizito is a school committed to providing a strong foundation for young learners. We offer a nurturing environment where children are guided towards academic excellence and personal growth.
