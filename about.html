Catholic Foundation

We nurture faith, discipline, and service through a values-centered education for every learner.

Holistic Learning

Our day and boarding programs develop academic excellence, life skills, and responsible leadership.

Strong Leadership

The school is guided by dedicated management and teachers committed to quality outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide quality and holistic Nursery and Primary Education that equips learners with knowledge, values, discipline, and life skills.

Our Vision

To be a national centre of excellence in the provision of Nursery and Primary Education.

Our Core Values

  • God-fearing conduct
  • Resilience and results orientation
  • Humility and integrity
  • Hard work and patriotism
  • Conservation of the environment

School Objectives

  • Instil discipline that guides learners beyond school.
  • Provide a quality academic foundation for the future.
  • Provide mentorship to strengthen leadership skills.
  • Shape confident learners with integrity and purpose.
Why Families Choose SKIPS

We combine strong academics, mentorship, values, and co-curricular opportunities to develop competent and compassionate learners.

Speech from the Head Teacher

At St. Kizito Primary School, Lira, we believe every child can grow into a confident, responsible, and compassionate learner. Our mission is to build strong academic foundations while nurturing values, discipline, and character through a safe and supportive school environment.

Together with our teachers, parents, and wider community, we remain committed to guiding each learner toward excellence in class and in life.

Head Teacher, St. Kizito Primary School
