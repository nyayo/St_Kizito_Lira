Academic Programs

Learning Pathways at St. Kizito

Our academic structure is designed to support every learner from foundational years through upper primary while building discipline, confidence, and practical life skills.

Early Childhood Education

We build a strong foundation through language exposure, number readiness, social development, and guided play in a safe and nurturing environment.

Lower Primary Section

Learners strengthen literacy, numeracy, curiosity, and classroom confidence through age-appropriate instruction and continuous support.

Upper Primary Section

Upper primary prepares pupils for academic progression with critical thinking, problem-solving, and consistent mastery of core subjects.

Our School Departments

Mathematics Department

Promotes logical reasoning, computation skills, and practical problem solving.

Science Department

Builds inquiry, observation, and experimentation for real-world understanding.

Language Department

Strengthens communication, reading, writing, and expression across contexts.

Social Studies Department

Develops civic awareness, cultural understanding, and social responsibility.

Extra-curricular Activities

The school exposes pupils to a number of indoor and outdoor activities that fall outside the normal curriculum to broaden educational experience and support affective, cognitive, and psychomotor growth.

These activities nurture confidence, responsibility, compassion, innovation, and collaboration, helping learners become well-rounded and socially responsible individuals.

St. Kizito Primary School

St. Kizito is a school committed to providing a strong foundation for young learners. We offer a nurturing environment where children are guided towards academic excellence and personal growth.
